Chapter 2: Iron Wolf

1445–1446 — Naming and expansion

In June 1445 the field army receives its formal name: Iron Wolf. Doctrine tightens, logistics improve, and a second army is planned to spread risk and extend reach.

1445 — Iron Wolf Established

Strength rises to 10,000: 5,000 infantry, 3,000 cavalry, 2,000 artillery.
