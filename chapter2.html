Chapter II — Expansion & The First War (1445–1446)

From cautious growth to open conflict: armies named and succession secured.

Overview

Mandate to Grow

Following the consolidation of power after 1444, Samogitia shifted from ad-hoc levies toward a structured field presence. In June 1445, the realm formalized its main field army as Iron Wolf under Daugvilas Tyzenhaus, reorganized to 5,000 / 3,000 / 2,000 (Inf/Cav/Art).

Chronology (1445–1446)

June 1445 — Iron Wolf designated and reorganized to 5k/3k/2k under Daugvilas Tyzenhaus.
January 1446 — Second field army formed: Black Death (5k/3k/2k) under Kantibutas Giedraiciai.
19 February 1446 — Heir born: Rolandas (stats 4 / 2 / 2). Royal marriage: Ausra.
