Chapter 2: Iron Wolf

1445–1446 — Naming and expansion

In June 1445 the field army is formally named Iron Wolf. Doctrine tightens, logistics improve, and plans form to field a second column.

1445 — Iron Wolf Established

Strength rises to 10,000: 5,000 infantry, 3,000 cavalry, 2,000 artillery.

1446 — Expansion & Dynasty

January 1446: a second regimented army, Black Death, is raised under Kantibutas Giedraiciai (OOB 5k/3k/2k).

19 February 1446: an heir, Rolandas, is born (stats 4/2/2). On the same day, a marriage is concluded with Ausra.

26 April 1446: Poland selects a Jagiellon to succeed Władysław III and enters a personal union with Lithuania. Both realms are ruled by Kazimierz Jagiellon (stats 4/4/1), with heir August Jagiellon.
