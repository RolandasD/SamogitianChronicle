Chapter 1: Dawn over Samogitia

1444 — The first formations

At the opening of 1444, Samogitia fields a modest but organized force. The army is a single field formation that later earns the name Iron Wolf, yet here it is simply the core of a fledgling state’s defense.

Order of Battle, 1444

The standing force totals 6,000 men: 5,000 infantry, 1,000 cavalry, and 0 artillery.
